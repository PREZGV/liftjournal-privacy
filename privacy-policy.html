Welcome to LiftJournal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application LiftJournal (the "App").

Workout Data

  • Workout dates and times
  • Custom workout routines

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the App

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We implement encryption, secure authentication, and regular security updates.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We only share data with Supabase (our hosting provider) and when required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct your information
  • Request account deletion
  • Export your workout data

7. Children's Privacy

LiftJournal is not for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of changes by updating the date above and posting in the App.
